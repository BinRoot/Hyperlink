Hyperlink.io

  • The hyperlink has been sterile since the beginning of the web.
  • Sometimes links are broken because they point nowhere.
  • Sites can change anytime, accidentally pointing you to a 404 page.
  • Spammy links are littered all over the internet.
  • It's 2013. Let's fix the hyperlink.

Introducting Hyperlink.io

Hyperlink.io provides a layer of abstraction for hyperlinks on the web. It detects when a link is broken or 404'd, and allows people to fix them.
