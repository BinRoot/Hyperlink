Hyperlink.js

  • The hyperlink has been sterile since the beginning of the web.
  • Sometimes links are broken because they point nowhere.
  • Sites can change anytime, accidentally pointing your link to a 404 page.
  • There needs to be a way to redirect URL without breaking existing hyperlinks.

Introducting Hyperlink.js

Hyperlink.js makes your links smart. It detects when a link is broken or 404'd, and allows web masters to update hyperlinks

By default, it colors broken links red, 404'd pages yellow, and redirected URLs blue.
